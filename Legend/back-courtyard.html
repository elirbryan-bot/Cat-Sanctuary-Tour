Back Courtyard / Pens

Sometimes when we get a colony of cats we will put them in a pen and close the gate so that they have a safe area to get used to being in the sanctuary. They get used to the coming and goings of the humans when it's time to eat dinner, etc. Once they are comfortable with life here, and when we open the gate to the back courtyard they consider that pen home. So even though they can come and go freely, they will go back to the pens at night and we know where to find them. The backs of the pens are closed off from visitors that the ferals have somewhere safe to hide and not be scared out of their homes by strangers when we're visiting. Pen 4,7 & 8 has a lot of feral cats that we need to medicate so we need to be able to find them quickly so we keep them in a closed pen so we can find them easily to medicate. They are also often set off by strangers coming into their space and the closed gate helps them to feel safe from people coming and going.
