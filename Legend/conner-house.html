Conner House

we have cages that the cats can stay in although small they are built to fit a human so that our staff and volunteers can work with the cats and lock themselves in. Once they're fully vaccinated, they get released, merely by us opening the door, and they're allowed to choose where they go. Some of them live up on top of the cages and some choose to find somewhere else to live. We generally leave the door open and there's a cat door so that they can come in and out if the weather is poor. The Connor was named after a 5 year old boy that died of cancer.  At his funeral,  his family asked for donations to the Sanctuary in his honor. The donations for Connor paid for renovations to the cabin.
