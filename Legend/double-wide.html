Double Wide/Hauser House

Double wide/Hauser house is named after Marcia Elizabeth Hauser, a cat lover who's family donated to honour Marcia's enduring love for all cats. This area is not open to the public. We have a lot of cats in cages becoming acclimatized to the sanctuary in Houser House and also have cats caged for surgery recovery and observation. It's also it's where our staff medical office is. The cages are larger and allow us to comfortably sit with the cats. Many of the cats that come to us as ferals become acclimatized to humans and even enjoy petting after awhile.
