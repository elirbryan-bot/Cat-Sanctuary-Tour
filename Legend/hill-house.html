Hill House

Hill House gets its name from cat-lover Stanley Hill, whose family made its refurbishing possible as a memorial, and he would have loved the bunch of cats who make themselves at home in it. The Hill house has larger cages for introducing cats to the sanctuary. The larger cages allow us to put a chair in there so we can sit with the new comers. This also allows us to cage multiple cats together which can ease their introduction to Sanctuary life.
