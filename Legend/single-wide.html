Single Wide

Singlewide is an area where we try to keep our diabetics and also any seniors that may be surrendered. It's a calm area that we create as a safe space for some of the cats that may not have as much mobility as the younger ones. Also, keeping the diabetics in a close proximity allows us to ensure that we have eyes on them when we're having to give their  medication. The cats in here don't have access to the outside but they do have an open air patio.
